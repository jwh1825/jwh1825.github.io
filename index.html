About

Hello! Welcome to my personal website that I created myself! I’m happy you have stopped by and are taking the time to learn a little bit about who I am, my background, and my experiences. So, let me introduce myself. I’m Jeffrey Hitchcock, a senior at the University of Michigan – Ann Arbor and an aspiring software engineer based in southeastern Michigan.

Ever since I was young, I’ve had a deep curiosity and interest in technology and computers. I would often be playing computer games, digging through the user customization settings, or even taking apart an old computer I found in the basement. This has led me to explore and study Computer Science in school, so I can enter the technology industry as I grow older. I love engaging in new challenges, pushing my boundaries, and continuously learning and growing.

When it comes to my work, excellence, growth, and learning are things I always strive for. I believe that every project, no matter the size, deserves genuine effort and careful attention to detail. This is why I always give anything I dedicate time to my most thorough effort and diligence.

I love engaging in new endeavors as every new situation will provide an opportunity for growth and learning, however much of my motivation comes from the opportunity to connect with new people. I always enjoy collaborating and building new relationships no matter the person or group. I believe teamwork, communication, and mutual respect are vital keys to success.

Outside of work, I live a balanced lifestyle and engage in activities that support both my physical and mental well-being. I take a great interest in seeing my friends and family, lifting weights, golf, snowboarding, and chess to name a few.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. Throughout the rest of this website, I aim to share my insights and experience with you. Whether you’re looking for a software developer or just simply viewing, I hope you enjoy your experience here. If you have any questions or would like to speak with me, please don’t hesitate to reach out!

Wishing you the best,

Jeffrey Hitchcock
